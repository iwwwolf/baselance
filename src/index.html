Baselance is a personal database of freelance professionals for studios, agencies, and producers

Baselance automatically syncs portfolios and contacts, knows how to sort freelancers by tags and categories, and can help you quickly put together a team for a new project.

Create your own freelancer database with just one click

To add a new freelancer to the database, you just need one click. Baselance will take care of carrying over their info and portfolio from social media networks and popular creative platforms. You can edit the newly created entry by adding tags, ratings, and comments.

Automatic updating

Baselance automatically updates database information. Should you add a freelancer from Behance, for example, our service will take care of syncing portfolio projects and contact information if these change.

Sort according to need

Tags and categories enable you to sort hundreds of individuals and quickly find the ones you need. You can add any kind of tag to an entry, like «flat design» or "works quickly".

No more Evernote, or Excel spreadsheets

Usually, a database like this is kept in Excel, Google Sheets, or Evernote. Baselance combines the strengths of these approaches, and leaves out their weaknesses.

1
Baselance automatically creates freelancer profiles.
No need to enter the data manually.
2
The database always contains up-to-date portfolios and contact information.
So you don’t need to track any changes.
3
It’s easy to find the freelancer you need by using their avatar or portfolio.
Much easier than trying to remember their last name or their username.
4
You can quickly sort freelancers by tags and categories.
Only Baselance and Evernote can do this! ;)
5
Share your contacts and entire collections with your colleagues using just one click.
So you can forget about the old copy & paste.
